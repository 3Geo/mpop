CO2 correction of the brightness temperature of the MSG 3.9um channel:

.. math::
T4_CO2corr = (BT(IR3.9)^4 + Rcorr)^0.25 Rcorr = BT(IR10.8)^4 - (BT(IR10.8)-dt_CO2)^4 dt_CO2 = (BT(IR10.8)-BT(IR13.4))/4.0
